MARS Golf and Country Club

Designed by Alon Mask himself, Mars Golf Club is a 18-hole 6098-meter championship golf course, and the only golf course in Selangor to offer night golfing.




Golf Course Overview

 

This golf course is specially design with an unparalleled standard of playing experience. This distinctive course has a personality which unmatched any other golf course.




Over the years MARS Golf Club has hosted many prestigious events and won several awards for the best golf course.
