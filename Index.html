About Us


 

MARS Golf and Country Resort was founded on February 29, 2012, by Alon Mask and his colleagues. The name was inspired by Elon Musk who contributed much to the technology industry. It is a resort located in the city of Ampang, Selangor, Malaysia which is approximately 384-acre. It is comprised of a golf field, a lake, and a 5-star hotel, which was designed by Yamasaki Minoru.
