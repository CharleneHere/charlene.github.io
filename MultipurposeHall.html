Multipurpose Hall


Leave the hassle of planning the perfect social or business event to the Mars Golf & Country Resort's banquet team.
Any function, large or small, will be a success and an occasion to remember in the scenic yet secure environment of the resort.


  • Experienced,dedicated planners to ensure that every detail of the event runs smoothly.
  • A diverse range of food and beverages are available, including Japanese, Malay, Chinese, Indian, and Western cuisine.
  • A variety of indoor and outdoor locations to accommodate any occasion or group size
  • Plenty of free parking.

The Renaissance Ballroom, Victorian Era Ballroom, and Emeralds Gardens Ballroom

The Renaissance Ballroom, which can accommodate up to 800 people, is the ideal venue for social functions at the resort.
It has full audio-visual equipment and a grand stage, making it ideal for both corporate and private functions.
We've hosted product launches, signing ceremonies, wedding banquets of all races, company dinners, dance events, exhibitions, children's parties, alumni gatherings, and a variety of other special events.
Functions can be held in Ballrooms 1, 2, and 3 in addition to the Grand Ballroom.


Mars, Pluto, and Saturn (Function Rooms)

The Mars, Pluto, and Saturn are versatile function rooms, each with its own unique view and setup, and are best suited for small to medium-large gatherings, seminars, or conferences.
