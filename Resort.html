What time is for the user to check in and check out?

Check in begins at 3:30 p.m. and check out ends at 12:00 p.m. During the booking process, you can request early check-in or late check-out. However, guests checking in or out before or after the designated period may be charged an additional fee.


What are the services that are provided?
