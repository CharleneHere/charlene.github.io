Lake



Water sports are good for both body and mind. It helps most people reduce stress, anxiety and depression.

Thus, our MARS Golf and Country Resort had provided various water sports for our visitors to have a fun experience at the lake.


Tubing
This is an activity where visitors can ride on top of an donut like tube while being pulled by a speed boat

Tubing Price

Choice Resort Customer Price (RM) Normal Price (RM)
Single rider 15 20
Couple rider 25 35
Multi-rider 60 80


Maximum capacity:
  • 1 person per single rider tube
  • 2 person per couple rider tube
  • 5 person per multi-rider tube

Safety tips:


  • Suitable for visitors that aged 5 years old and above (Except Kayaking)
  • Kayaking: Suitable for visitors that aged 10 years old and above
  • Always wear a personal flotation device that will be provided before the ride
  • Have basic knowledge of the equipment used
  • Bring water-proof whistle or mobile phone in a water-proof bag
  • Warm up before the activity
  • Check everything once again before you start your journey
  • Keep any valuables in a water-proof bag

Operating Hours:


8 am - 6:30 pm daily (Subject to weather condition)
